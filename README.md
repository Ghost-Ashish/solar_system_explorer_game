## Scientific Accuracy

### Real Data Included
- **Earth**: 1 AU from Sun, 365.25-day orbit, 12,742 km diameter
- **Moon**: 384,400 km from Earth, 27.3-day orbit, 3,474 km diameter
- **Mars**: 1.52 AU from Sun, 687-day orbit, 6,779 km diameter
- **Sun**: 1,392,700 km diameter, 99.86% of solar system mass

### Physics Simulation
- **Kepler's Laws**: Planets orbit in ellipses (simplified to circles for visualization)
- **Orbital Periods**: Proportional to actual orbital speeds
- **Relative Speeds**: Inner planets orbit faster than outer ones
- **Scale Compression**: Distances compressed for visualization while maintaining ratios
